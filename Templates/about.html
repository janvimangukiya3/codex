About This Project

Understanding the technology behind accurate delivery time predictions

Problem Statement

Food delivery platforms operate in highly dynamic urban environments where delivery time is influenced by multiple factors such as delivery distance, order characteristics, delivery partner efficiency, and environmental conditions. Accurate delivery time estimation is critical for customer satisfaction, resource planning, and operational optimization.

Traditional estimation methods rely on average delivery times or static distance-based rules, which fail to capture real-world variability and non-linear relationships between influencing factors.

Our Solution

We developed an intelligent machine learning system that predicts delivery time using a Random Forest Regressor trained on historical delivery data. The model considers multiple features including:

  • Delivery partner age and experience
  • Delivery partner ratings
  • Delivery distance in kilometers
  • Type of vehicle used
  • Type of order being delivered
